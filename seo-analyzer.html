Why Use a Text Analyzer?

Creating high-quality, engaging content is crucial for both user retention and search engine optimization (SEO). Whether you are crafting a blog post, an academic essay, or a social media update, knowing your text metrics ensures you hit your target guidelines. Our free SEO Text & Readability Analyzer helps writers optimize their text instantly without the need for expensive software.

How to Use This Tool

Using the analyzer is incredibly simple. Just copy your text from your word processor and paste it directly into the input box above. The tool utilizes advanced browser-side scripts to instantly calculate your metrics in real-time as you type or paste.

Because the processing happens locally on your device, your text is never uploaded to any server, ensuring your unpublished drafts and private documents remain 100% secure and confidential.

Understanding Your Metrics

  • Word Count: Essential for hitting SEO length targets, freelance writing assignments, or academic essay requirements. Search engines often favor comprehensive content.
  • Reading Time: We calculate this based on the average adult reading speed of 238 words per minute. Including a reading time on your blogs can reduce bounce rates by setting clear expectations for your visitors.
  • Character Count: Vital for crafting the perfect SEO meta descriptions (typically under 160 characters) and ensuring your social media posts fit within platform limits.
  • Sentence Count: Helps you gauge the pacing of your text. Too few sentences in a high word-count text usually indicates overly long, complex sentences that hurt readability.
